HOW TO ADD YOUR MEDICAL IMAGES
================================

Please save your 6 medical images in this folder (public/images/) with these exact names:

1. doctor-consultation.jpg
   - The image showing a doctor with patient/medical consultation
   - Used in: Features Section (Expert Orthopedic Care)

2. medical-facility.jpg
   - The image showing a doctor with stethoscope/medical professional
   - Used in: Features Section (State-of-the-Art Facilities)

3. digital-health.jpg
   - The image showing medical injection/treatment procedure
   - Used in: Features Section (Digital Health Management)

4. joint-pain.jpg
   - The image showing ankle/foot joint pain (blue/red illustration)
   - Used in: Services Section (Joint Replacement Excellence)

5. knee-pain.jpg
   - The image showing running figure with knee pain (blue illustration)
   - Used in: Services Section (Sports Medicine & Performance)

6. shoulder-pain.jpg
   - The image showing shoulder/elbow pain (blue/red illustration)
   - Used in: Services Section (Comprehensive Pain Management)

STEPS:
------
1. Rename your images to match the names above
2. Copy all 6 images to this folder: public/images/
3. Refresh your browser at http://localhost:3000
4. Your images will now appear on the landing page!

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
-----------------
- JPG/JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PNG (.png)
- WebP (.webp)

RECOMMENDED SIZE:
----------------
- Width: 800-1200px
- Height: 600-900px
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or 16:9
- File size: Under 500KB each (optimized for web)

NOTE: The images are already configured in the HTML with proper styling,
lazy loading, and responsive design. Just add your images and they'll
work perfectly!
