Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our healthcare portal

üöÄ Getting Started

How do I create an account? +
Click the "Register Now" button on the homepage and complete the 6-step registration process. You'll need to provide your citizenship information, personal details, location, contact information, and create a secure password. The process takes about 5-10 minutes.
What documents do I need to register? +
For Kenyan citizens, you'll need your National ID or Passport number. Foreign nationals need a valid Passport, while residents need an Alien ID. You'll also need your KRA PIN number and valid contact information (phone and email).
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email. The reset link is valid for 24 hours.

üìÖ Appointments

How do I book an appointment? +
Log into your account, navigate to the Appointments section, select your preferred doctor and specialty, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email and SMS.
Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment? +
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Go to "My Appointments," select the appointment, and choose "Reschedule" or "Cancel." Please note that late cancellations may incur a fee.
Will I receive appointment reminders? +
Yes, you'll receive automatic reminders via SMS and email 24 hours before your appointment, and again 2 hours before. You can customize reminder preferences in your account settings.

üìã Medical Records

How do I access my medical records? +
Log into your account and navigate to the "Medical Records" section. You can view your complete medical history, test results, prescriptions, and treatment plans. Records are updated in real-time after each visit.
Can I download or print my records? +
Yes, you can download your records as PDF files or print them directly from the portal. Each document includes a verification code for authenticity.
How secure are my medical records? +
Your records are protected with enterprise-grade encryption and stored on secure servers. We comply with all healthcare data protection regulations. Only you and authorized healthcare providers can access your records.

üíä Prescriptions

How do I view my prescriptions? +
Go to the "Prescriptions" section in your dashboard. You'll see all active and past prescriptions, including dosage instructions, refill information, and pharmacy details.
Can I request prescription refills online? +
Yes, you can request refills through the portal. Select the prescription, click "Request Refill," and your doctor will review and approve it. You'll be notified when it's ready for pickup at your chosen pharmacy.

üí∞ Billing & Payments

What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and insurance payments. All online payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
How do I view my billing history? +
Navigate to "Billing & Payments" in your dashboard to view all invoices, payment history, and outstanding balances. You can download receipts and invoices as PDF files.
Does the portal accept insurance? +
Yes, we work with major insurance providers in Kenya. Add your insurance information in your profile, and we'll process claims directly. You'll only pay any applicable co-payments or deductibles.

üîí Security & Privacy

Is my personal information safe? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL) to protect all data transmission and storage. Our systems undergo regular security audits and comply with international healthcare data protection standards.
Who can see my medical information? +
Only you and healthcare providers directly involved in your care can access your medical information. You can view an audit log of who has accessed your records and when.
Can I enable two-factor authentication? +
Yes, we strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security. Go to Security Settings and follow the setup instructions. You'll receive a verification code via SMS or authenticator app each time you log in.
