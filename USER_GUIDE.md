## üöÄ Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the System
1. Open your web browser
2. Go to: **http://localhost:3000**
3. You'll see the landing page with information about the portal

### Step 2: Create Your Account

#### Option A: Register as New User
1. Click **"Register Now"** button on the landing page
2. You'll go through a **6-step registration process**:

**Step 1: Citizenship Information**
- Select your citizenship type:
  - ‚úÖ Kenyan Citizen
  - ‚úÖ Foreign National  
  - ‚úÖ Resident
- Choose your ID type based on citizenship:
  - National ID (for Kenyan citizens)
  - Passport (for foreign nationals)
  - Alien ID (for residents)
- Enter your ID number

**Step 2: Personal Information**
- First Name
- Last Name
- Date of Birth
- Gender (Male/Female/Other)
- Marital Status (Single/Married/Divorced/Widowed)
- Occupation
- KRA PIN number

**Step 3: Location Information**
- Country: Kenya (default)
- County: Select from dropdown (e.g., Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu)
- Constituency: Auto-populated based on county
- Ward: Auto-populated based on constituency
- Physical Address: Your street address

**Step 4: Contact Information**
- Email Address
- Phone Number (format: +254 700 000 000)
- Alternate Phone Number (optional)

**Step 5: Dependants (Optional)**
- Add family members if needed:
  - Spouse
  - Children
  - Parents
  - Other relatives
- For each dependant, provide:
  - Name
  - Relationship
  - Date of Birth
  - ID Number (if applicable)

**Step 6: Security**
- Create a strong password:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - At least 1 uppercase letter
  - At least 1 lowercase letter
  - At least 1 number
  - At least 1 special character
- Confirm password
- Accept Terms and Conditions

3. Click **"Register"** to complete
4. You'll be automatically logged in and redirected to the dashboard

#### Option B: Login with Existing Account
1. Click **"Login"** on the landing page
2. Enter your email address or ID number
3. Enter your password
4. Click **"Sign In"**

## üìÖ How to Book an Appointment

### Step 1: Access Appointments
1. From dashboard, click **"Manage Appointments"**
2. You'll see 3 tabs: **Book Appointment**, **Upcoming**, **Past**

### Step 2: Book New Appointment
1. Click **"Book Appointment"** tab
2. Fill in the form:

**Select Specialty:**
- Orthopedics
- Sports Medicine
- Spine Care
- Joint Replacement
- Trauma Care
- Pediatric Orthopedics

**Choose Doctor:**
- Dropdown will populate based on selected specialty
- Each specialty has different doctors available

**Select Date:**
- Choose your preferred appointment date
- Must be today or future date

**Choose Time Slot:**
- Available slots will appear after selecting doctor and date
- Slots are 30-minute intervals from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- Unavailable slots are grayed out

**Appointment Type:**
- Consultation (first visit)
- Follow-up (return visit)
- Routine Checkup
- Emergency

**Provide Details:**
- Reason for Visit (required): Describe your symptoms or concern
- Symptoms (optional): List specific symptoms

3. Click **"Book Appointment"**
4. You'll receive confirmation email and SMS

### Step 3: Manage Appointments
**View Upcoming Appointments:**
- Click **"Upcoming"** tab
- See all scheduled appointments
- Each shows: Date, Time, Doctor, Type, Status

**View Past Appointments:**
- Click **"Past"** tab
- See completed, cancelled, or no-show appointments

**Cancel Appointment:**
- Click **"Cancel"** button on any upcoming appointment
- Provide cancellation reason
- Must cancel at least 24 hours before appointment

**Reschedule Appointment:**
- Click **"Reschedule"** button
- Currently shows message to cancel and book new one
- (Full reschedule feature can be added later)

## üìã Managing Medical Records

### Step 1: Access Medical Records
1. From dashboard, click **"View Records"**
2. You'll see the medical records page

### Step 2: Upload Documents
1. **Drag and Drop Method:**
   - Drag files from your computer to the upload area
   - Supports PDF, JPG, PNG files up to 10MB

2. **Click to Upload Method:**
   - Click the upload area
   - Select files from your computer

3. **Add Document Details:**
   - Document Type: Lab Result, Prescription, X-Ray, Medical Report, Other
   - Title: Give your document a name
   - Description: Optional description
   - Document Date: When the document was created

4. Click **"Upload Document"**

### Step 3: View and Manage Documents
**Filter Documents:**
- By Type: All Types, Lab Results, Prescriptions, X-Rays, Medical Reports, Other
- By Date: All Time, Last Week, Last Month, Last Year
- By Search: Type keywords to search

**View Documents:**
- Click **"View"** button on any document
- Opens document viewer (functionality can be enhanced)

**Download Documents:**
- Click **"Download"** button
- File downloads to your computer

## üí∞ Billing and Payments

### Step 1: Access Billing
1. From dashboard, click **"View Billing"**
2. You'll see your outstanding balance at the top

### Step 2: View Invoices
**Outstanding Balance Card:**
- Shows total amount you owe
- Click **"Pay All"** to pay everything
- Click **"View Outstanding"** to see unpaid invoices

**Invoice Tabs:**
- **All Invoices**: Every invoice ever created
- **Outstanding**: Unpaid or partially paid invoices
- **Paid**: Fully paid invoices

**Invoice Information:**
Each invoice shows:
- Invoice number (e.g., INV-000001)
- Issue date and due date
- Total amount and balance due
- Status (Pending, Paid, Partially Paid, Overdue)
- Payment method (if paid)

### Step 3: Make Payments
1. Click **"Pay Now"** on any invoice
2. **Select Payment Method:**

**M-Pesa:**
- Enter your M-Pesa phone number
- Format: +254 700 000 000

**Card Payment:**
- Enter card number (1234 5678 9012 3456)
- Enter expiry date (MM/YY)
- Enter CVV (3-digit code)

**Bank Transfer:**
- Select this option for bank transfer
- You'll receive bank details

3. Click **"Pay Now"**
4. Payment will be processed
5. You'll receive confirmation
6. Invoice status updates to "Paid"

### Step 4: View Payment History
- Paid invoices show in the "Paid" tab
- Each shows payment method and date paid
- You can download receipts (feature can be enhanced)

## üí° Tips for Best Experience
